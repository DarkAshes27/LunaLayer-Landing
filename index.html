How It Works

Present when you need it.
Invisible when you don't.

LunaLayer sits quietly on top of your annotator — a lunar layer between your calendar and your craft.

✨

See Google Calendar on top of your planner

A translucent panel floats over any annotator — GoodNotes, Noteshelf, Xodo. Your workspace stays full screen, undisturbed.

✨

Toggle ghost mode to write underneath

Enable ghost mode and your S Pen or stylus passes straight through. The overlay stays visible while your writing stays free.

✨

Fully customizable layout

Colors, fonts, transparency, grid — everything adapts to match your planner's aesthetic, not the other way around.

✨

Live Google Calendar sync

Your actual events, right inside your planner view. No copy-paste, no manual re-entry, no gap between your digital life and your pages.

✨

Auto-launch with your planner app

Set LunaLayer to appear automatically when you open your annotator. Your session starts complete — no extra taps.

✨

Built natively for Android

Not a web wrapper. A native Android WindowManager overlay — stable, fast, and built around how Android actually works.
